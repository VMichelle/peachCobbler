Our History

About our founder Philip Peach

In 1912, Philip Peach, age 18, left Arad, Hungary, to join his older brother, Joseph, living in Atlanta, Georgia. He sailed on the Prinz Freidrich Wilhelm from Bremen, Germany. After a 12-day voyage to New York he traveled to Atlanta by train. His brother talked a local cobbler into taking Philip as his apprentice and the rest is history
